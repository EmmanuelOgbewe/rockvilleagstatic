Giving & A Tithe

Acts verse about giving

Giving is a bibical principle practiced from the early church through today. God calls us to each give a tenth of what he has given us back to him. For the portion of our time and talents that is monitary, Rockville Church provides a method of giving electronically. Feel free to subsitute check, cash and other forms of transaction with the online portal. Need to pay for kids camp? Yup, good to go. You can do it from here. If the item you're looking for is not listed in the pull download, select other and fill in the memo line so we can direct your giving appropriately.
