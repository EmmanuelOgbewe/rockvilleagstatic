What you can expect when you attend or visit Rockville Assembly of God

"One Church, Many Nations"

Rockville Assembly of God is a loving congregation of Christ followers that live full lives committed to demostrating this love to Rockville, Maryland as well as the rest of the world.

Jesus clearly stated that the gift of salvation is avaliable to EVERYONE that calls on his name, and obeys his commands. [John 14:23, John 14:6] At Rockville Assembly of God, we invite you to celebrate this radical love, and lasting joy with us as we grow to be more Christ like.

Service Times

Sundays, we meet at 9:30 for prayer, fellowship and Sunday school classes as they are offered.

Music celebration begins promptly at 10:30, followed by the Word of God delivered by Sr. Pastor Stan Nelson.

Child care is provided for all ages under 9. If you have any questions or special needs, please connect with us here.
